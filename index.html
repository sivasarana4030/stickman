Highway Traffic Unblocked

By Classroom Games

 

Traffic might not seem like the most exciting thing in the world, but playing Highway Traffic on Classroom Games could change your mind. In this game, your mission is to drive down the highway, skillfully dodging other cars and avoiding accidents. The challenge comes from the unpredictable behavior of other vehicles, which may slow down, speed up, or stop abruptly. You’ll need to stay alert and be ready to make quick maneuvers. Patience is key—this isn't about racing, but about carefully navigating through traffic. The longer you avoid collisions, the more points you earn and the more upgrades you can unlock.

Game Controls

  • W or Up Arrow to accelerate
  • A/D or Left/Right Arrow to steer the car
  • Space Bar to use the handbrake
  • Left Shift or F to use car boost
